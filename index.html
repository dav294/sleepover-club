Who We Are

Sleepover Club is an independent music promoter based in Ireland and the UK, curating unforgettable live music experiences since 2018. With a passion for supporting both emerging and established talent, we've hosted 50 events across Dublin, London, Belfast, and Galway, featuring over 120 acts from Ireland, the UK and beyond.

From intimate gigs to sold-out events, we bring together music lovers and creatives to celebrate the vibrant music scenes. Stay tuned for more incredible nights of live performances, artistic collaborations, and unforgettable moments.
